About us

TADIKA CERIA GEMBIRA is a preschool educational institution for children. It was established in the year 2003. We teach social experience for children for their transition from home to school. Playing, activities, experience, and social interaction are accepted as essential aspects of developing skills and knowledge. We value play as an opportunity for learning as a child discovers how to communicate and interact with others whilst learning new skills and developing creativity and imagination.


Mission: Provide a whole-child education in terms of child development in the areas of intellectual, physical, language, social and emotional through concept building, hands-on work, musical involvement, art with imagination and drama.


Vision: To help build a better society through educating and developing children to be useful members of the society.
