Crocker Range

>Crocker Range

Crocker Range (Malay: Banjaran Crocker) is a mountain range in West Coast Division of Sabah, Malaysia that separates the west and east coast of Sabah. At an average height of 1,800 metres (5,906 ft), it is the highest mountain range in the state with the range is named after the British administrator in North Borneo, William Maunder Crocker.
