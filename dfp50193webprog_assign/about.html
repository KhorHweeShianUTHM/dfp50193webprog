GEOGRAPHY OF MALAYSIA

The geography of Malaysia deals with the physical and human geography of Malaysia, a Southeast Asian country.

There are two major parts to this country, Peninsular Malaysia to the west and East Malaysia to the east.

MOUNTAIN

Peninsular Malaysia contains numerous mountain ranges running parallel from north to south along the peninsula.

Mountain ranges in the East tend to follow north-south or northeast-southwest paths, and the highest ranges from the border between Malaysia and Indonesia.

FOREST

Malaysian forests can be categorised as tropical rainforest.

Malaysia's rainforest's are made of a variety of types, mainly dipterocarp, swamps, and mangroves.
